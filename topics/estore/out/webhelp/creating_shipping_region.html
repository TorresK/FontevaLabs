Creating a Shipping Region

The Shipping Region is defined using country names—not states, provinces, cities, or postal codes. When an order contains items that require shipping, customers must enter a shipping address in the eStore checkout. The destination country, item weight or item value, and the item quantity determine the shipping rates displayed to shoppers.
  • Create the Shipping Carrier and Delivery Method
  1. Navigate to the Shipping Regions tab in the eStore app and click New.
  2. Enter the Shipping Region Name.
    The Shipping Region Name appears in the Shipping Rates lookup. Optionally, enter a Display Name and Description.
  3. Enter the countries within the named Shipping Region into the Address Field Values (CSV) field. Click Save.
    Best practices recommend including any common abbreviations for country names. For example, customers may enter "USA", "US", or "United States" for the shipping country. Consequently, it is important to list all variations of the country name in the Address Field Values (CSV) field so the customer can complete the purchase. Country names are not case sensitive.
The Shipping Region is created and any countries entered into the Address Field Values (CSV) field will populate the Shipping Rates lookup.

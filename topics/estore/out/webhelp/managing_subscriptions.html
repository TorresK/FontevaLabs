Managing Subscriptions in the Community Portal

Authenticated portal users can manage their subscriptions directly from the Community Portal..
  • Purchase a subscription through the backend or the Community Portal.
  1. Log in to the Community Portal and click the Subscriptions tab in the side navbar.
  2. Click on the Manage button to modify the subscription:
    • Change Assignments via the Manage Members tab:

      Only assignees with the Is Primary and Is Active checkboxes enabled on the Assignment Role record can modify assignments via the Role picklist.
      Note: Enable Assignments must be checked on the Subscription Item record. Disable the Restrict Assignments Visibility field on the Account record to view assignments of other Contacts on the Account; default is unchecked.

      Click the +Add New Contact button and enter the First Name, Last Name, and Email to assign the subscription to any additional contacts. Click Save.

    • Disable Auto Renew Option and Update Payment Methods via Manage Renewal tab:

      Select an alternative Payment Method via the Payment Method for Auto Renewal picklist. To disable the Auto Renew Option, click the Cancel Renewal button.

By managing their subscription in the Community Portal, an authenticated portal user can change assignments, disable the Auto Renew Option, and update their Payment Methods.

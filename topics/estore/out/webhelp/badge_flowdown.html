Badge Flowdown

Badge Flowdown allows users to assign a badge to a Parent Account and allow the Child Accounts and their related Contacts to receive any benefits of the badge. Badge Flowdown is composed of four primary components:
  • Enabling Badge Calculations
  • Enabling Badge Flowdown
  • Disabling Badge Flowdown for specific Accounts
  • Badges VisualForce Component on the Contact Detail Page
Badge records are not created for the Child Accounts and their Contacts. The system populates the Badges VisualForce Component on the Contact record for reporting purposes.
Note: In order to view all the badges benefiting a Contact, the user must add the Badges Visualforce Component to the Contact record's page layout.

Enabling Badge Calculations

To enable Badge calculations, navigate to the Spark Admin tab in the Spark Framework app and click Apps.

Next, click Charge and activate the Enables Badge Flow Down update. This enables the calculation of badges from the Badges related list on the Contact record to the Badges field on the Contact record.

Enabling Badge Flowdown

To enable Badge Flowdown:
  1. Navigate to the Badge Type to be enabled for flowdown.
  2. Check the Enable Badge Flowdown checkbox and click Save.
    Note: Type must be set to Line Account.

Disabling Badge Flowdown

The Disable Badge Flowdown checkbox allows users to define specific accounts that cannot receive benefits if flowdown badges are assigned to their parent accounts. To disable badge flowdown:
  1. Navigate to the Account to be removed from badge flowdown.
  2. Check the Disable Badge Flowdown checkbox and click Save.

Badges Field and VisualForce Component

Records are not created for all Contacts when badges are assigned to an Account, nor are badges created for Child Accounts and their Contacts when an Account receives a badge that is enabled for badge flowdown. To report on assigned badges, users can use the Badges field on the Contact record.

Badges are available to display based upon three relationships:
  1. Direct relationships are badges that are directly assigned to the contact record.
  2. Inherited relationships are the badges that are assigned to the related account record.
  3. Flowdown relationships are the badges that are assigned to the related accountâ€™s parent account and where the badge is enabled for flowdown.

    The Badges field is a Long Text Area field that concatenates every badge that gets assigned to a Contact, Account, or Parent Account when the Enable Badge Flowdown checkbox is checked. To view badges assigned to a contact (inherited, direct, and flowdown) navigate to the Contact record.

Badges Tab in the Community Portal

To display Badges in the Community Portal, navigate to the Spark Admin tab in the Spark Framework app and click Apps.

Next, click Engage Member Community and activate Displays My Badges tab on my profile page.

When logged in to the Community Portal, contacts can click on the Badges tab to view their associated badges.

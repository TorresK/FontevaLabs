Creating Campaign Pages for Catalog Items

Community Portal users can purchase items through both the Shopping Cart and the related campaign pages. Staff users can design a site to promote and sell existing fundraisers, subscriptions, memberships, and events through picklists, radio buttons, custom text and labels. The published site links to the selected Event, Store, Sales Order, and Sales Order Line. Publishing a campaing page also allows staff users to showcase the revenue generated by an item.
Note: In the event an organization is linking a product to an image, it is critical that all images originate from a secure site. Some browsers, like Google Chrome, will not allow linking to an unsecured site.
  • Check the associated Item Attribute and the Is Active checkboxes on the related Item Class record.
  • Create a Store
  • Create a Catalog with Catalog Items
  1. Navigate to the Campaign Pages tab in the eStore app and click New.
  2. Enter the Campaign Name and identify the Store, Payment Gateway, and Theme from the lookups. Click Save.
    Select the Shopping Cart Enabled checkbox to link the campaign page to the Shopping Cart for the related Store.
  3. Click the Campaign Page Builder button.
  4. Drag-and-drop the desired component from the sidebar to the main body of the page. Select the catalog from the picklist. Click Save.
    Configure the fields in the sidebar Settings to customize the campaign page. Click Refresh to review and click Preview to inspect the page.
  5. Click Publish.
    The Is Published checkbox is enabled upon returning to the campaign page record.
  • Click the Go Back button to return to the Campaign Page record.
  • Navigate to the Item Campaign Page by clicking the Community URL link.

Adding Bar Codes and Identifiers

Staff users have the ability to add bar codes and other identifiers such as SKU, UPC, EAN, and ISBN numbers on items.
Adding Bar Codes & Identifiers
  • SKU - A SKU (Stock Keeping Unit) is a unique Item identifier that references attributes associated with the Item and that distinguish it from all other Items primarily used for inventory control and management.
  • UPC - A UPC (Universal Product Code) is a 13 digit catalog number uniquely assigned to an Item required to generate UPC barcodes.
  • EAN - An EAN (European Article Number) is a bar code symbology consisting of a unique 13-digit Global Trade Item Number for identifying non-book products in Europe.
  • ISBN - An ISBN (International Standard Book Number) is a unique 10 or 13-digit Global Trade Item Number used to identify books and publications.

Creating a Lifetime Subscription Plan

A Lifetime Subscription Plan, by default, will have a term of 100 years beginning on the purchase date.
Note: A Lifetime Subscription Plan cannot be set up for the Auto Renew Option.
  1. Navigate to Subscription Plans in the eStore app and click New.
  2. Enter a Name for the Subscription Plan, set the Type to Lifetime, and check the Is Active checkbox.
  3. Add a Description and click Save.
A Lifetime Subscription Plan is created.

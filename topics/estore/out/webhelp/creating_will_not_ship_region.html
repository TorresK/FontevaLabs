Creating a "Will Not Ship" Region

Admins can create a Will Not Ship region to identify a country that will not be able to receive shipping items. When enabled, the Will Not Ship feature displays a custom message to the shopper indicating that the region they are trying to ship to is not supported; the shopper is prevented from completing their purchase.
  • Create the Shipping Carrier and Delivery Method
  • Create the Shipping Region
  1. Navigate to the Shipping Regions tab in the eStore app and click New.
  2. Enter the Shipping Region Name.
    The Shipping Region Name appears in the Shipping Rates lookup. Optionally, enter a Display Name and Description.
  3. Enter the countries that will not be able to receive shipments within the named Shipping Region into the Address Field Values (CSV) field.
    Best practices recommend including any common abbreviations for country names. For example, customers may enter "USA", "US", or "United States" for the shipping country. Consequently, it is important to include each into the Address Field Values (CSV) field so the customer can complete the purchase. Country names are not case sensitive.
  4. Check the Will Not Ship checkbox.
  5. Enter a Will Not Ship Message to display to customers attempting to ship purchases to an unsupported destination. Click Save.
Shoppers will not be able to ship to any region designated as Will Not Ship. If a customer tries to ship to a country in this region, they will see the custom message and are prevented from completing the purchase.

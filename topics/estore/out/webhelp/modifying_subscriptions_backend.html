Modifying Subscriptions

Upgrading or downgrading a subscription will renew an existing, active subscription with a different subscription item and Subscription Plan.
  1. Navigate to the eBusiness app, click the Sales Orders tab, and click New.
  2. Create a Sales Order with Receipt as the Posting Entity and Simple Receipt as the Schedule Type. Click Save.
  3. Navigate to the Sales Order Lines related list and click the New Sales Order Line button.
  4. Select a different subscription item using the Item lookup.
  5. Check the Is Renewal checkbox and select a different Subscription Plan using the lookup.
  6. Select the original subscription using the Subscription (Upgrade/Renew) lookup. Click Save.
  7. Click the Sales Order ID and update the Status to Closed.
  8. Complete payment on the Sales Order.
    1. If the Posting Entity is set to Receipt, payment is required in order to complete the renewal request. Click the Sales Receipt button, enter payment information, and click Pay. The Posting Status on the Sales Order record automatically updates to Posted to reflect payment.
The original subscription will reflect a Status of Upgraded and the new subscription will have a Status of Pending until the Current Term Start Date begins.
Admins can additionally:
  • Amend Subscriptions
  • Renew Subscriptions
